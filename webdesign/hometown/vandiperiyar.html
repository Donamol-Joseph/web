About Vandiperiyar

Vandiperiyar is a beautiful town located in the Idukki district of Kerala, India.It is known for its scenic beauty and tea plantations. Surrounded by lush green forests and hills, it is a perfect place for nature lovers and tourists seeking peace and tranquility.

Location

  • The town is situated at an altitude of 1,300 feet above sea level, offering a cool and pleasant climate throughout the year.
  • 18 km from Thekkady

    • Known for

  • The area is famous for its agricultural products, especially tea and spices.
  • Vandiperiyar is well-connected to nearby tourist attractions like Thekkady, Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, and the famous Periyar Lake.

    • Attractions

  • Visitors can enjoy activities like trekking, boating, and exploring the vibrant culture of the region.
  • Rose garden,Flowering Plants,Vegetable markets...
    • Whether you are looking to relax amidst nature or embark on an adventurous journey, Vandiperiyar offers something for everyone.
