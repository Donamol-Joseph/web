Computer Science and Engineering

The Dept of Computer Science and Engineering is one of the best units that has always enhanced the glory of AJCE. Over the years, we have been successful in preparing young minds to be outstanding engineers who are prominent in academics and related skills. The Dept is equipped with a team of dedicated and dynamic faculty who work effortlessly to bring the best out of students. In addition to classroom learning, the students are provided with various training programmes which include paper presentations, technical seminars and mock- interviews, prior to the selection interviews. From video games to hospital monitoring equipment, computer systems have evolved to become an inevitable part of human life. We assure you that learning Computer Science at AJCE will give you a solid background in engineering fundamentals and opportunities to work on projects with reputed researchers who represent the cutting-edge of Computer Science.
